GETTING STARTED

There are 2 ways to join Fyre Skin Brand Partnership Program.

  1. Join directly from the Fyre Skin website by clicking the link. Register
  2. Join using the link of a brand partner that recommended you and they automatically become you Up-line partner and you their Down-line partner.

If you decide to join, the startup registration is 25,000 Naira with a monthly renewal of 10,000 Naira.

Your monthly subscription gives you access to

  • Your own back office dashboard were you can see your commission, payments, pending payments, bonuses, sales made using your link, clients who bought using your link, team members profile, team members sales and profit and marketing tools .
  • Your own personalise Fyre Skin website that your potential clients can shop with.
  • Your personalised brand partnership link for potential clients .
  • Your personalised Networking link for building your own team.
  • Growth and development training for personal growth.
  • A community of entrepreneurs that you can learn from.

How to get started - For us at Fyre Skin convenience is everything that why we built a website that’s swift and easy to access by both brand partners and clients.

  1. After signing up and creating an account, login into your back office the click copy ‘default brand partners link’. Share your link on social platforms that can be visible potential clients.
  2. This links leads clients directly to your own Fyre Skin website where clients have access to buy products and book services.
  3. Immediately after clients payment, you are alerted via email that a purchase has been made using your link and on your back office you can see your commission on ‘pending’ till we confirm payment from clients.
  4. After that it reflects in your back office that your clients purchase has been ‘approved’.

Payout period - This refers to the length of time that the residual income is paid out. In fyre skin our brand partners are paid every Friday. The payout is a combination of all sales made from Friday - Friday . The total payout a brand partner will receive is reflected on the ‘approved’ layout on their dashboard after which that would change to ‘paid’ commission on their dashboard.

NETWORKING

Networking is the process of recruiting people and making a commission from each person you recruit. This process helps Fyre Skin increase sales force and helps you build a team that comprises of your downlines whom you’re going to help secure financial freedom by mentoring them. The beauty of our networking scheme is you only get a one-off 20% Recruitment bonus from people whom you directly recruit and you make nothing from anyone they recruit. You can recruit as many people as you want using your own link.

Please Note: Unlike the regular Network marketing pyramid scheme that requires you to bring a lot of people in the 3-legs formation in order for you to make money and move up in rank, leaving those at the bottom with nothing, the Fyre Skin brand partnership program is designed in a unique model that makes sure you don’t need people in order for you to make an income or move up in rank. What we offer is the direct networking, you only get a commission for anyone whom you directly recruit and you have no commission from any one your downlines recruits .

How networking works: Brand partners make 20% from signup fee per every person they recruit. Signup fee is 25,000.

Ade in his one month tester period, he was able to signup 8 people in the space of 26 days.

20% of 25000= 5000

5000 x 8 people = 40,000

Ade made 40,000 in recruitment bonus.

Networking is choice it doesn’t affect your rank

Registration fee: A ₦25,000 one-off fee paid in order to be accepted into the program.

Re-subscription Fee: A monthly ₦10,000 fee paid in order to remain in the program.

Personal Retail Sale Commission (PRSC):Total sales in commission made by a brand partner

Compensation Plan: This includes 20% commission in marketing, 20% from signup fee in networking and 10% commission per sales a direct recruit makes in commission bonus.
